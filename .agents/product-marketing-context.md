# Colocsy — Product Marketing Context

## What We Do
Colocsy is a roommate-matching platform for India that uses personality-based compatibility scoring to match people looking to live together. Users fill out a lifestyle survey, swipe on ranked matches (0–100 compatibility score), and when two people mutually like each other, a shared group chat and property wishlist are automatically created. No broker fees, ever.

## Tagline
"Find roommates you'll actually like."

## Core Value Proposition
Traditional roommate search in India means broker fees, random Facebook groups, or living with strangers you know nothing about. Colocsy removes all of that — no broker fees, no cold DMs, just compatibility-matched people who share your lifestyle.

## The Problem We Solve
- Broker fees eat 1–2 months of rent on top of security deposits
- Existing solutions (NoBroker, FB groups, OLX) match on budget/location only — not lifestyle compatibility
- Moving in with an incompatible roommate is one of the most stressful life experiences for young professionals and students

## Target Audience

### Primary: Young Urban Professionals (22–30)
- Relocating to Bangalore, Mumbai, or Hyderabad for work
- First or second time living away from home
- Budget: ₹10,000–₹30,000/month rent
- Care about: cleanliness habits, work schedules, social preferences, dietary compatibility
- Pain point: Can't afford to make a bad roommate decision — the cost of moving again is huge

### Secondary: Students & Interns
- Moving to a new city for college or internship
- Budget-conscious (₹5,000–₹15,000)
- Often need to move fast (ASAP or 1-month timeline)

## Key Features
- **5-minute lifestyle survey** — budget, location, move-in timeline, smoking, pets, diet, sleep schedule, social battery, work style
- **Compatibility score (0–100)** — ranked swipe queue, not random browsing
- **Mutual match system** — both must like each other to connect (no one-sided cold messages)
- **"Liked You" tab** — see who liked your profile and like back
- **Group chat + property wishlist** — shared space to coordinate apartment hunting
- **Shared expense tracking** — kit items, debts between roommates
- **Zero broker fees** — ever

## Positioning
**Category:** Roommate matching / co-living discovery
**Against:** NoBroker (property-focused, not people-focused), Facebook groups (no vetting, no compatibility), OLX (transactional, no matching)
**Key differentiator:** We match on *who you are*, not just *where you want to live* and *what you can afford*
